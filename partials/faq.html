How to login

Click the login button in the nav bar. this will direct you to the login page. You can type in your username and password to login.

Can anyone look at jobs?

Yes anyone can.

Can anyone post a job?

Anyone can post a job as long as they have made an account and logged in.

How to register

Click the register button on the top right corner and fill out the form.

Can I edit a job posting?

If it is your job posting then yes. You cannot edit anothers. To edit, login, go to My account and select My Postings. Find the posting you want to edit and click edit.

Cand I delete my posting?

Yes you can. To delete the posting, login. After that go to My Account. Next Step is to click My Postings and find the posting you want to delete and press delete
